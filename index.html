FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I download the Android application? +
You can download the APK package directly by clicking "Download Android App" in the hero section above. Once downloaded, tap the file on your device and permit installation from "Unknown Sources" if prompted.
Is the platform completely free to use? +
Yes, Echo of Baduga is entirely free and non-profit. There are no payment gates, subscriptions, or intrusive third-party advertisements.
Where are the audio files hosted? +
Our database pulls audio streams dynamically. Large catalog additions are integrated using secure cloud file servers to guarantee high delivery speeds and reliable uptime.
Can I suggest new songs to upload? +
Absolutely! We welcome contributions. You can contact us using the contact form below or reach out directly on Instagram to request specific songs or artists.
